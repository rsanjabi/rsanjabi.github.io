About

A computer scientist by training, I LOVE data - pipeline building, exploring, visualizing, or machine learning. I especially enjoy full-stack problems, from gathering data through deploying solutions. I speak Python, SQL, scikit-learn, and pandas. When I’m not playing with data, I like to garden, meditate, or write.

I’m currently seeking opportunities as a data engineer or data scientist in San Francisco or remote.

Why You Should Hire Me

  • Ability to self-teach While I have BS in Computer Science, I’ve spent the last year bringing my skills up to modern standards and specializing in data concerns. I can handle the uncertainty that comes with changing times.
  • Curiosity I enjoy trying to understand what the data says or asking if there is a better way to engineer solutions. Solving problems are fun; looking deeper is even more fun.
  • Wholistic Having an eclectic professional background, including design training and management, has given me a broad experience base that helps when it comes to keeping business needs at the forefront of decision making.
